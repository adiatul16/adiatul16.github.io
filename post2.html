Redmi Note 9 Pro: A Short Review

Posted by Aditya Ramakrishnan on October 12, 2020

Design

The Redmi Note 9 Pro is a bulky phone with a thickness of 8.8mm and weighs 209g however Xiaomi claims that the weight has been evenly spread out and it is important to say that the phone cannot be used single handedly.

This phone uses Gorilla Glass 5 both front and back while the frame is made of polycarbonate.Even though it is made of sturdy strong glass it easily attracts fingerprints.

The rear panel has an noticeable camera bump but the camera placements syncs with the Aura Design and the flash is placed below the camera.

On talking about the front panel we have a Punch Hole Camera placed in the centre of the display however the choice of panel Xiaomi choose was LCD rather than AMOLED.On considering the price the display seems a perfect fit for this phone.

On the right side of the phone Fingerprint Scanner/Power Button and Volume rockers are present.Here the volume rockers are placed in an awkward which is too far for small hands to touch.

On the left side of the Phone we have SIM Card Slot which supports two SIM cards and a Micro SD Card simultaneously.

On the top end of the phone we have Xiaomi's Infrared Sensor and Notification LED and in the bottom we have a USB C Port, 3.5 mm HeadPhone Jack, and a downward firing mono speaker

Xiaomi has done a pretty good job in terms of design even though there are minor flaws in it. There's also P2i coating for splash resistance.

Display

The Redmi Note 9 Pro comes with an 6.67-inch (16.94 cm) LCD screen with IPS display which is pretty good for gaming and movie watching experience however the colours here are not as vibrant as AMOLED screens.Here Xiaomi have gone with 20:9 aspect ratio thus reducing the size of the corner bezels.The pixel density in this display is 395 ppi and can support resolution upto 1080 x 2400 pixels.

Camera

The Redmi Note 9 Pro consists of a Quad Camera System with the 48 Megapixel primary camera,8 megapixel wide angle camera,5 megapixel macro camera and 2-megapixel depth sensor.

Under good lightning conditions the photos taken with this camera are bright and sharp and has an good dynamic range. The real issue starts when it comes to low light conditions the photos are noisy.On the front we have a 16-megapixel selfie camera which can tak some pretty good shots.
